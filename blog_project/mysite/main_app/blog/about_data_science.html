Data Science : Sexiest Job of 21st Century

Jatin Pandey
Co-Founder, CEO (skillds.com)
August 28, 2020

There is lot of buzz about Data Science on social media and other platforms. Experts define Data Scientist as a person who is better at programming among other statisticians and better at statistics among other programmers. In simple terms, data science can be thought as combination of programming and statistics. In broader view, it is a complex subject. Data Scientist working in insurance industry may require different knowledge compared to the Data Scientist working in medical field. Here, We will discuss about the overview of Data Science.

What Should I Learn to be a Data Scientist

A data scientist should be familiar with several programming languages to achieve different tasks required for data science. SQL knowledge might be needed to extract data from relational databases. Python can be used to perform data loading, transformation, and analysis tasks. MATLAB or PowerPoint to present information.. It may not be possible to rely on one programming language to carry all the task but Python has been widely used throughout the data science community to load data, transform it, analyze it and even present it to the end-user. A data scientist may have to choose other languages to fill their toolkit. Most popular languages which are used across this filed is Python, R, SQL, Java, and Scala. To stand out from the crowd, it’s important that you know what to put on a resume to quickly demonstrate your ability. Otherwise, it might get thrown into the “no” pile before the hiring manager sees the full extent of your experience. .

Let's know some cool Applications of Data Science:
  • Image Recognition, Image Classification.
  • Speech Recognition, Sentiment Analysis, other NLP tasks.
  • Recommender System.
  • Fraud And Detection System.
  • Targeted Advertising.
  • Health care, Medical Image Analysis, Drug Development, Genetics And Genomics.
  • Augmented Reality.
  • Self Driving Cars.
  • Self Driving Cars.

A lot more to come. Stay tuned! Enjoy learning!!
